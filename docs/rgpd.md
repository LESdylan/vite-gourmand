## When to provide this information

This information must be provided to individuals at the time their personal data is obtained, or if the personal data is not obtained from the individual, within a reasonable period after obtaining the data, but at the latest within one month. If the personal data is to be used for communication with the individual, the information must be provided at the latest when the first communication takes place. If the personal data is to be disclosed to another recipient, the information must be provided at the latest when the personal data is first disclosed.

- [ ] If you are using cookies or similar tracking technologies, you must provide clear and comprehensive information about the use of these technologies, including the purposes for which they are used and how individuals can manage their preferences.

- [ ] If you are collecting personal data from children, you must provide clear and age-appropriate information about the processing of their data, and obtain parental consent where required.

- [ ] If you are processing personal data for direct marketing purposes, you must provide clear and concise information about the processing activities, including the right to object to such processing.

- [ ] If you are transferring personal data to a third country or international organisation, you must provide information about the safeguards in place to protect the data during the transfer.

- [ ] If you are using personal data for automated decision-making, including profiling, you must provide information about the logic involved, as well as the significance and potential consequences of such processing for the individual.

  - [ ] Written in clear and plain language, especially if addressed to a child (easily accessible and understandable)

## Changes to this privacy notice
We may update this privacy notice from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy notice on this page. You are advised to review this privacy notice periodically for any changes. Changes to this privacy notice are effective when they are posted on this page.

- [ ] we regularly review our privacy notice to ensure it remains accurate and up-to-date, and we will update it as necessary to reflect any changes in our data processing practices or legal requirements.

- [ ] We put ourselves with the users shoes to make sure that the information we provide is clear and understandable, and we are always open to feedback to improve our communication about privacy matters.

- [ ] we carry out testing to evalute how effective our privacy notice is in conveying the necessary information to users, and we make adjustments based on the results of these tests to enhance clarity and accessibility.

## Best practice to user

When providing information to user about how we process their personal data, we follow these best practices:

- [ ] a lay out the information in a clear and organized manner, using headings and bullet points to make it easy to read.

- [ ] dashboards
- [ ] we use plain language and avoid technical jargon to ensure that the information is easily understandable by a wide audience.
- [ ] just-in-time information: we provide information at the point of data collection or processing, so that users have the necessary context to understand how their data will be used.
