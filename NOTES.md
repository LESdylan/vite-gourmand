Nice—10 days in New York City can involve a lot of walking, changing weather, and varied plans, so packing smart really pays off. Here’s a thorough, practical packing list broken into categories so your luggage stays organized and you don’t overpack.

🧳 CORE DOCUMENTS & ESSENTIALS
Passport (valid, with copies—paper + digital)
Visa / ESTA (if applicable)
Boarding passes / flight info
Travel insurance documents
Hotel / accommodation confirmations
Credit/debit cards + some USD cash
Driver’s license (if you might rent a car)
Emergency contacts list
👕 CLOTHING (10 DAYS)

(Adjust slightly depending on season, but layering is key in NYC)

Basics
7–10 underwear
7–10 pairs of socks
2–3 bras (if applicable)
Pajamas
Tops
5–7 T-shirts or casual tops
2–3 nicer shirts (for dinners / going out)
1–2 long-sleeve tops
Bottoms
2–3 pants/jeans
1–2 skirts or shorts (seasonal)
Layers (VERY important for NYC)
1 light jacket
1 warmer layer (sweater/hoodie)
Compact rain jacket or umbrella
Shoes
1 pair comfortable walking shoes (CRUCIAL)
1 nicer pair (optional)
Flip-flops/slippers (optional)
🧥 WEATHER-SPECIFIC (check forecast before leaving)
Scarf (light or warm depending on season)
Gloves (winter)
Sunglasses
Hat or cap
